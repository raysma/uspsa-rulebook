Appendix A2 · pages – USPSA Recognition Prior to the commencement of a match, the organizers must specify which Division(s) will be recognized. Unless otherwise specified, USPSA sanctioned matches will recognize Divisions and Categories based on the number of registered competitors who actually compete in the match, based on the following criteria: 1. Divisions Level I/Level II/Level IIIA minimum of 5 competitors per Division (recommended) NationalsA minimum of 10 competitors per Division (mandatory) 2. Categories Division status must be achieved before Categories are recognized. All level matchesA minimum of 5 competitors per Division Category (see approved list below) 3.
