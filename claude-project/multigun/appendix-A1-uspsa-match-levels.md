Appendix A1 · pages – USPSA Match Levels Level I — Club matches conducted on a regularly scheduled basis throughout the year. Level II — Sectional and State matches conducted on an annual basis. Level III — Annual championship matches conducted by USPSA Areas (1 through 8). Nationals — Annual championship matches conducted by USPSA. Note: Major matches conducted on an irregular or annual basis and which do not represent a specific Section, State, or Area Championship shall request either Level II or Level III Sanctioning.
