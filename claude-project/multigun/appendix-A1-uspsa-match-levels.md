# USPSA Multigun Rules — Appendix A1: USPSA Match Levels

> Per the 2026 USPSA Multigun Rules. Cite as "Appendix A1 — USPSA Match Levels".
> Note: tables and diagrams from the source are captured as text only.

Level I — Club matches conducted on a regularly scheduled basis throughout the year. Level II — Sectional and State matches conducted on an annual basis. Level III — Annual championship matches conducted by USPSA Areas (1 through 8). Nationals — Annual championship matches conducted by USPSA. Note: Major matches conducted on an irregular or annual basis and which do not represent a specific Section, State, or Area Championship shall request either Level II or Level III Sanctioning.
