Match Directors have the latitude to set the award schedules based on the number of competitors within a division/class once minimums are met. For example, the Match Director may elect to use the 5/7/9 approach where 1st place is recognized if there are 5 entries, 2nd place if there are 7, and 3rd place if there are 9. Alternatively, a single multiple such as 5 may be used. All category awards along with Steel Master and Rifle Master awards are limited to a single award for High Overall. A2.1 Category Awards All competitors compete for overall placement in the match. In addition to overall placement, competitors may also compete for category-specific awards based on personal characteristics. Competitors may enter more than one category. For example, an individual may qualify for (and enter) both the Junior and Lady categories. Category Eligibility Applicable Documentation Lady A competitor individually registered as “Lady” is a person who was registered female at the time of birth and who has two X chromosomes (XX).
