# Steel Challenge (SCSA) Rules — Appendix A1: Match Levels

> Per the 2026 Steel Challenge (SCSA) Rules. Cite as "Appendix A1 — Match Levels".
> Note: tables and diagrams from the source are captured as text only.

Level III (Regional/ Level IV (World Championship) Must use official Steel Challenge Stages Level I (Local) Level II (State) National) R (A minimum of 2 M M M official stages must be used) Certified Range Master N/A R R* M Range Master approved by NROI N/A M M M Competitors must be SCSA members R M M M -Mandatory -Recommended * Note: If Range Master is not certified, NROI approval required. Special Notes: Level II matches may elect to run only 6 of the 8 Steel Challenge stages, and may omit ONLY Outer Limits and Speed Option, due to distance limitations. Level III matches must run all 8 Steel Challenge stages. Level II and higher require prior approval – NO match may use the SCSA name without being conducted at/by a SCSA affiliated club/ range. Matches represented to be a “SCSA Championship” (State, etc.) must be Level II or above.
